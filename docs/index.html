Spiral Chess

K players take turns. Each walks an outward spiral from its own starting cell and drops a piece on the first cell not attacked by the other players' pieces. Square lattice is based on OEIS A392177 / A392178; the hexagonal lattice is the same game on a hex grid. Pick the lattice, the number of rounds, the players, their pieces, colors and starting indices (position along the spiral, origin = 0), then press Run. A player marked "protected from" an opponent ignores that opponent's threats — it may place on a cell that opponent only attacks (no pieces are ever removed). The piece's leaper vector is shown after its name. Randomize fills every control at random and runs.
